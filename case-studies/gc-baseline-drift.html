GC Baseline Drift After Calibration

Analyzer · Gas Chromatograph · Calibration

Plant / Environment

Refinery – Continuous analyzer shelter.

Problem Statement

After routine calibration, GC baseline started drifting slowly, affecting measurement accuracy.

Observed Symptoms

  • Baseline drift over time
  • Unstable detector response
  • Frequent recalibration required

Root Cause

Moisture contamination in carrier gas due to exhausted dryer.

Corrective Action

Replaced carrier gas dryer and purged system. Baseline stabilized after corrective action.

Lessons Learned

  • Monitor dryer life cycle
  • Verify gas purity before calibration
  • Schedule preventive replacement
