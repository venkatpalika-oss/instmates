GC8000 Error 603 – Oven Heater Failure

Oven temperature control failure during analyzer startup.

Case Snapshot

  • Analyzer: Yokogawa GC8000
  • Error Code: 603
  • Application: Refinery – Process Unit GC
  • Status: Analyzer not coming online

Observed Symptoms

  • GC stuck in startup sequence
  • Error 603 displayed after warm-up attempt
  • Oven temperature remained at ambient
  • No temperature rise even after restart

Initial Actions Taken

  • Analyzer restarted multiple times
  • Method reloaded from backup
  • Controller reset performed

These actions did not clear the alarm because the failure was hardware-related.

Detailed Diagnosis

  1. Oven temperature trend monitored – no increase observed
  2. Heater supply voltage checked – present
  3. RTD resistance measured – normal (~110 Ω at ambient)
  4. Heater output current measured – zero

Root Cause Identified

The oven heater relay on the oven control board failed to switch, preventing heater power from reaching the oven element.

Corrective Action

  • Oven control board isolated and removed
  • Heater relay confirmed faulty
  • Oven control board replaced
  • Analyzer restarted and monitored

Result After Fix

  • Error 603 cleared automatically
  • Oven reached setpoint within normal warm-up time
  • GC returned to stable analysis

Failure Logic (Simplified)

Lessons Learned

  • Error 603 is typically a hardware failure
  • Healthy RTD does not guarantee heater operation
  • Heater current measurement is critical
  • Repeated restarts do not fix heater circuit faults
