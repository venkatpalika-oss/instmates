GC8000 Error 604 – Oven Overtemperature

Oven temperature exceeded safe operating limit and protection activated.

Case Snapshot

  • Analyzer: Yokogawa GC8000
  • Error Code: 604
  • Application: Refinery – Process Analyzer Shelter
  • Status: Analyzer tripped to protect oven

Observed Symptoms

  • Oven temperature increased rapidly beyond setpoint
  • Error 604 triggered during operation or warm-up
  • Analyzer entered protective shutdown
  • Heater output remained ON despite shutdown command

Initial Actions Taken

  • Analyzer powered OFF immediately
  • Heater supply isolated for safety
  • Alarm history and temperature trends reviewed

Immediate shutdown prevented oven damage and column degradation.

Detailed Diagnosis

  1. Oven temperature trend confirmed runaway heating
  2. RTD resistance checked – normal and stable
  3. Heater control signal measured – stuck ON
  4. Heater relay continuity checked – relay contacts welded

Root Cause Identified

The oven heater relay was mechanically stuck in the ON position, causing continuous heating regardless of controller output.

Corrective Action

  • Oven heater supply isolated
  • Oven control board removed
  • Faulty heater relay confirmed
  • Oven control board replaced
  • Temperature control re-validated

Result After Fix

  • Error 604 cleared after restart
  • Oven temperature stabilized at setpoint
  • No further overtemperature events observed
  • Analyzer returned to normal operation

Failure Logic (Simplified)

Lessons Learned

  • Error 604 is a serious safety-related fault
  • RTD health does not rule out heater runaway
  • Relay failure can cause uncontrolled heating
  • Immediate power isolation prevents equipment damage
