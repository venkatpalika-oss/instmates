Analyzer Failure Case Study

Analyzer · Instrumentation · Field Experience

Plant / Environment

Refinery – Continuous operation unit.

Problem Statement

Analyzer output was unstable and showing abnormal readings.

Observed Symptoms

  • Signal fluctuation
  • Frequent alarms
  • Calibration failure

Root Cause

Moisture ingress into sample line due to damaged insulation.

Corrective Action

Sample line replaced and insulation improved.

Lessons Learned

  • Always verify sample conditioning
  • Check insulation during shutdowns
