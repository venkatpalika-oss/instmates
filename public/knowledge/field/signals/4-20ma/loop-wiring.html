4–20 mA Loop Wiring Types

Transmitters are classified based on how they are powered and how the signal loop is formed.

Two-Wire (Loop Powered)

Most common field instrument wiring.

Three-Wire Transmitter

Separate power return, common signal ground.

Four-Wire Transmitter

External power supply. Signal loop independent of power.

Field Rule

Always confirm wiring type before replacing a transmitter.
