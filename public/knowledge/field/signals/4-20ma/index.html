4–20 mA Signal – Field Instrumentation

The 4–20 mA current loop is the backbone of industrial instrumentation. It is used to transmit pressure, temperature, flow, level, and analyzer measurements reliably across long distances and harsh plant environments.

Field Rule

If a 4–20 mA loop is wrong, always verify the loop current with a multimeter before blaming the transmitter, PLC, or DCS configuration.
