Field Wiring & Grounding

Practical wiring and grounding guidance for instrumentation technicians, based on real plant installations and failures.

Shielding Best Practices

  • Ground shield at one end only
  • Avoid floating shields
  • Keep signal and power cables separate

Grounding Principles

  • Single-point grounding philosophy
  • Avoid multiple earth paths
  • Bonding is as important as grounding

Common Wiring Failures

  • Loose terminals in junction boxes
  • Incorrect gland termination
  • Improper cable armor grounding

Field Symptoms

  • Intermittent signal loss
  • Noise only during heavy load operation
  • Multiple instruments failing together
