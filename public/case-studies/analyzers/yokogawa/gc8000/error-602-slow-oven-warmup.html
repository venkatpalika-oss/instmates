Typical Field Symptoms

  • Oven temperature increases very slowly
  • Startup timeout leading to Error 602
  • Analyzer remains stuck in warm-up sequence
  • No chromatographic analysis initiated

Common Suspected Causes

  • Partial degradation of oven heater
  • Low or unstable heater supply voltage
  • Restricted airflow or blocked ventilation
  • Heat loss due to aging insulation
  • Incorrect oven temperature ramp parameters
