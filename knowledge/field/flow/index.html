Flow Measurement – Fundamentals

Practical flow measurement knowledge for technicians, focused on installation effects, impulse lines, transmitter behavior, and real plant problems.

Common Flow Measurement Methods

  • Differential pressure (orifice, venturi, flow nozzle)
  • Magnetic flowmeters
  • Vortex flowmeters
  • Mass flowmeters (Coriolis, thermal)

Differential Pressure (DP) Flow – Field Reality

  • Impulse line routing affects accuracy
  • Condensation causes false low or high flow
  • Incorrect tapping orientation creates bias
  • Manifold leakage causes unstable readings

Common Field Problems

  • Flow reading changes with ambient temperature
  • Zero shift after shutdown
  • Flow stable locally but wrong at DCS
  • Plugged impulse lines
