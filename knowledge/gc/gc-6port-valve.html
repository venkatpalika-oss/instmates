GC 6-Port Sampling Valve – Basics

A 6-port valve is used in gas chromatographs to load a fixed volume of sample into a sample loop and then inject it into the column with carrier gas. Understanding valve position and timing is critical for repeatability.

6-Port Valve – Load & Inject Operation

In the LOAD position, the sample loop is filled with process gas. In the INJECT position, the valve rotor rotates and carrier gas sweeps the sample into the column.

Key Points for Technicians

  • Sample volume is defined only by the sample loop.
  • Valve timing affects repeatability and accuracy.
  • Leakage or sticking causes retention time shift.
  • Incorrect valve position leads to zero or unstable peaks.
