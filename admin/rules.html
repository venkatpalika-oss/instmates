Moderation & Verification Rules

Content Guidelines

  • Technical accuracy is mandatory
  • No unsafe or unverified procedures
  • No promotional or vendor bias
  • Respectful professional communication

Verification Criteria

  • Minimum field experience evidence
  • Clear problem–cause–solution structure
  • Peer validation preferred

Enforcement

  • Warnings for minor violations
  • Content removal for repeated issues
  • Account suspension for misuse
