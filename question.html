GC baseline drifting after calibration – possible causes?

Asked by Venkatesh · Analyzer · GC

After calibration of GC, baseline is drifting slowly. Carrier gas pressure is stable. What could be the root cause?

Answers

Check detector temperature stability and column conditioning. Baseline drift often occurs due to moisture in carrier gas.
